Eulerians

  • Score
    Eulerians are ranked on scores which are earned by winning a position, p, within the first fifty members in the ten most recent fastest solvers tables. For each scoring position, 51 − p is calculated and the five best scores are added. By hovering the mouse over the performance bar you will be able to see how the points have been allocated.
  • Performance Bar
    Peformance is a separate metric, but it does not affect the score. It is determined by using the member's five best scores: for each table they appear within the first fifty they earn 10% and each top ten position earns an additional 10%.
